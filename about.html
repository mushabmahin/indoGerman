About Indo-German Academy

Learn about our mission, immersive Goethe-standard teaching methodology, and direct pathways to Germany.

Our Mission & Approach

Indo-German Academy committed to helping students reach their language goals in an effective and enjoyable manner. Operating out of Malappuram and Ernakulam, Kerala, we provide a complete pipeline for students wishing to pursue higher education or career placements in Germany.

We specialize in teaching German at all competence levels, from beginner (A1) to advanced intermediate (B2). Our course structures prioritize conversational immersion, vocabulary retention, and Goethe-Institut syllabus alignment.

Beyond the language classroom, we manage candidate visa files, university placement directories, block account setup, accommodation search, and pre-departure cultural orientation.

Immersive Learning

Pronunciation drills, roleplay dialogues, and vocabulary integrations.

Certified Trainers

German experts with native fluency and exam certification guidelines.

Exam Confidence

Mock tests modeled after Goethe and TELC testing standards.

Visa & Account Assistance

Step-by-step guidance through blocked accounts, visas, and university approvals.

Our Course Levels

We follow the Common European Framework of Reference for Languages (CEFR) standard for language levels.

A1

Beginner

Learn basic expressions, vocabulary, and introductory greetings.

A2

Elementary

Communicate in simple, routine tasks and express daily routines.

B1

Intermediate

Describe dreams, goals, reasons, and speak in connected text paragraphs.

B2

Upper Intermediate

Understand complex texts, converse fluidly with native speakers, and handle career environments.
